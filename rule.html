RULES

DOTA 2

    NOTE

  1. Show proof of transfer and participant ticket to the committee.

  2. If a participant is 10 minutes late or does not attend the match then it is considered WO.

    GROUP STAGE

    32 groups consisting of four teams, each match consisting of 1 match against the same opponent. A 1-0 win gives 3 points, and 0-1 gives 0 points. Each group will pass 1 team with the highest points.

    TIEBREAKERS

  1. If there are equal points between teams in 1 group, the number of kill deaths from all matches in the group played will be compared.

  2. If there are points and the same kill death will be compared to the number of the fastest match duration of all matches in the group being played.

    ROUND OF 32

    The first place in each Group Stage group will enter the 32 teams that qualify from each group will be competed with a knockout (KO) system. The team that wins 2-0 or 2-1 will qualify for the round of 16. The order in which the teams will be shuffled in this round.

    *) All games in the Round of 32 are Best of 1.

    ROUND OF 16

    Teams that qualify from the round of 32 will enter the last 16, the teams that qualify will be faced with a knockout (KO) system. The team that wins 2-0 or 2-1 will qualify for the last 8. The order in which the teams will be shuffled in this round.

    *) All games in the Round of 16 are Best of 1.

    MAIN EVENT

    8 teams that passed the last 16 will be competed according to the results of the shuffle.

    UPPER BRACKET

  • Upper Bracket A: Team 1 vs Team 2

  • Upper Bracket B: Team 3 vs Team 4

  • Upper Bracket C: Team 5 vs Team 6

  • Upper Bracket A: Team 7 vs Team 8

    • Round: Selection Priority for the first game is determined by the toss of a coin. Alternative to game two. A coin toss is used again when there is a third play.

    *) All Upper Bracket games are Best of 3. Grand Final is Best of 5.

    LOWER BRACKET

  • Lower Bracket A: Team Lose to Upper Bracket A vs Team Lose to Upper Bracket B

  • Lower Bracket B: Team Lose to Upper Bracket C vs Team Lose to Upper Bracket D.

    • Round: Selection Priority for the first game is determined by the toss of a coin. Alternative to game two. A coin toss is used again when there is a third play.

    *) All Lower Bracket games are Best of 3.
